Privacy Policy

Petra Baptist Church (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects information.

Information We Collect

The Petra Baptist Church App does not require users to create accounts, log in, or provide personal information to use its features. The app may include links to external websites such as YouTube, Facebook, or Bible study tools. When you follow those links, the privacy policies of those external services apply.

How We Use Information

Any information you voluntarily submit through forms or links (such as email or contact forms) is used solely to respond to your request or inquiry.

Third-Party Services

The app may display content from or link to third-party services (for example, YouTube, Facebook, or BibleGateway). We do not collect or store any information from those services. Please review each third-party provider’s privacy policy for their data practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you provide. However, no mobile application or internet transmission is completely secure. Use of this app is at your own discretion and risk.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this address.
