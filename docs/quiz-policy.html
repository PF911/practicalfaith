Privacy Policy
Bible Quiz Challenge

Effective Date: March 13, 2026
Bible Quiz Challenge is a free, ad-free ministry app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains what the app stores and how it is used.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Bible Quiz Challenge does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, or any other personal data.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide a better experience, the app saves certain information directly on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. It includes:

  • Your quiz scores and performance history
  • Daily challenge progress and streak count
  • Bookmarked Bible verses
  • Incorrectly answered questions saved for practice
  • Your selected quiz settings and preferences

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device's settings.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

Bible Quiz Challenge does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not use cookies or similar tracking mechanisms.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Because we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to share with third parties. We do not sell, rent, trade, or disclose any user information.

5. Children's Privacy

Bible Quiz Challenge does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any user, it is safe for all ages. If you believe a child has inadvertently provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will address the concern promptly.

6. Score Sharing

The app includes an optional "Share Your Score" feature. If you choose to share your score, your device's built-in sharing system (such as iOS Share Sheet or Android Share) is used. Any information shared is handled by the app or platform you choose to share to, not by us. Sharing is entirely voluntary.

7. Links to External Sites

The app contains a link to PracticalFaith.net. If you visit that website, it may be governed by its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review any third-party privacy policies before providing personal information.

8. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no personal data is transmitted to any server, your information remains under your own control and the security protections of your device's operating system.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
